Course title:

2

Prerequisites:
No prior knowledge is required to complete this course.
Objectives:

The main objectives are:

-To Learn about the origin and evolution of the city of Madrid from the creation in the VIII century until today.

-To analyze the most emblematic buildings in the city, on which we will work drawing them and trying to know their history.

-To learn about the Spanish and Madrilenian culture
