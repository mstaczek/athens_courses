Prerequisites:
Electromagnetism, Basic Structure of Matter, Foundations of Computing
Objectives:
  • Presentation of Additive Manufacturing as an advanced manufacturing technology
  • Presentation of Laser-Based Additive Manufacturing
  • Presentation of basic mechanisms in Laser-Based Additive Manufacturing
  • Presentation of process design guidelines in Laser-Based Additive Manufacturing
  • Presentation of representative applications of Laser-Based Additive Manufacturing

 


Programme to be followed:

Day 1: Additive Manufacturing Foundations. ISO/ASTM Process Classification. Panoramic view of Additive Manufacturing applications. (T)

Day 2: Laser-Based Additive Manufacturing. Processes description and design guidelines. (T)

Day 3: Process Design and Modelling tools for Laser-Based Additive Manufacturing (T)

Day 4: Development of practical examples of Process Design in Laser-Based Additive Manufacturing (P)

Day 5: Laboratory demonstrations on quality control procedures for pieces built by Additive Manufacturing (P) + Final Test.
