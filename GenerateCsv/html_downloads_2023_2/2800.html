Objectives:

The objectives of the program are : 

1) Learning by doing - apply lean innovation and lean entrepreneurship principles to a small group (3-4 person) startup project (real or imagined).

2) Applying the principles of customer development, product development and business model development to your group's startup project.

3) Using design thinking to develop solutions to real-world problems for real-world customers

4) Understanding startup finance and how to develop a viable revenue model and business model

5) Learning to create a prototype and pitch innovation projects Silicon Valley style.
