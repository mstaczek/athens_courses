Course title:
5th centenary of the first circumnavigation of the World
Course code:
UPM133
Minimum year of study:
1
Language of tutition:
English
 
Prerequisites:
None
Objectives:

Introduce the students to this important milestone in Spanish history, a key event in universal history
Programme to be followed:

Lessons

  1. The world at the beginning of S.XVI. The discovery of America
  2. The Portuguese and Spanish empires. The Treaty of Tordesillas 1494
  3. The economic impact of international spice trade. Moluccas Islands
  4. Commerce and Navigation
  5. An opportunity for the epic. Objectives of the expedition
  6. The organisation of a maritime expedition. Economic factors
  7. The Capitulations. 22 March 1518
  8. The ships
  9. The crew and the important people
  10. A voyage or an adventure. 10 August 1519 - 8 September 1522
  11. Epilogue. Consequences and new world order

Visits

  1. Naval Museum – “We were the first” exposition
  2. National Center for Geographic Information – “The maps and the first circumnavigation of the World. Magellan and Elcano’s voyage”
  3. AGNYEE – Replicating the adventure 2019-2022– PROS ship (virtual visit)

Lectures

  1. Targeting micro plastics in the oceans
  2. Teamwork – cooperative, gets along with other, supportive, leadership
  3. Communication – oral speaking, presenting, writing
Course exam:

Team work and presentation
