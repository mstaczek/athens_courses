Course title:
Next Generation Photovoltaics for High Efficient Energy Conversion
Course code:
UPM96
Minimum year of study:
2
Language of tutition:
English
 
Prerequisites:

Students of physics and engineering. A minimum background on semiconductor physics and devices will make the course more enjoyable.

Objectives:

The objective of this course is to provide an overview of the advanced technologies that are being developed nowadays for boosting the efficiency of PV devices for energy conversion applications (solar, thermal and solar-thermal)

Programme to be followed:
  1. Fundamentals of PV energy conversion
  2. Silicon solar cells
  3. Multijunction solar cells
  4. Novel PV concepts for solar energy conversion
  5. Concentrating PV (CPV) systems
  6. Thermophotovoltaic (TPV) systems
  7. Market forecasts for PV and CPV technologies
  8. Visit to the laboratories of the Instituto de Energía Solar
