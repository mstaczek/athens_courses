Course title:
Milan, the unexpected green-growing city. A view from inside.
Course code:
POLI19
Minimum year of study:
2
Language of tutition:
English
 
Prerequisites:

None, just to be motivated and curious! And to be not afraid by public transports, walking and cycling…

Objectives:

International students are called to discover, analyse and discuss through lectures and guided visits Milan as a city where gardens, parks and greenery have always been part of its continuous transformation during its millenary history. This unusual point of view, focusing on environmental design and landscape architecture techniques, permits to understand better the town hosting EXPO 2015 and the relation that connects the Milanese public open spaces to its urban planning evolution and to the green and sustainable skyscrapers that have recently changed its silhouette.
