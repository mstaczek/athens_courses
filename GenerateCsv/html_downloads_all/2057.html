Course title:
Energy scenario and Climate Protection

Prerequisites:
Basic knowledge in energy fields
Objectives:
In this course different primary energy sources will be analyzed for replacing the fossil fuels. The main objective of the course consists to analyze different energy scenario from economic, technical, technological, and environmental point of view. Thus, economic, environmental and technical criteria will be proposed for creating a multi-criteria model used to choose the optimal energy scenario.

6.       Renewable energy sources

7.       Long Term Energy Scenario,

8.       Energy Pricing,

9.       Energy Sector Reforms,

10.   Energy and Environment

11.   Air Pollution,

13.   Energy Security,
