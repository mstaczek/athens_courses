Objectives:

The objective of the course is to introduce students into energy efficient building design, focusing on innovative heat production using renewable energy sources and waste heat utilization. Energy management in town-scale energy communities will be explained on the example of modern district heating. Building service engineering systems, fit for nearly zero energy buildings will be explained as well as intelligent building automation systems in order to create smart buildings.
