Objectives:

Become conscious of the processes regarding environmental design, city and landscape metabolism, sustainability, and urban ecology. Students will be work in groups and will produce a three-minute video-clip to be presented at the final discussion on the lectures and visits contents.

International students are called to discover, analyze, and discuss through lectures and guided visits Milan as a city where gardens, parks, and greenery have always been part of its continuous transformation during its millenary history. Focusing on environmental design and landscape architecture techniques, this course introduces – also to students without any architectural or landscape architectural background – the relevant meaning of these multi-layered open space infrastructures inside a contemporary urban metropolis.
