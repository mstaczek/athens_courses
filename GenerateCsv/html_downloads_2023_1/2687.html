Course title:
Finite Element Modeling of Vibrating Elastic Structures
Type of course:
Classic ATHENS course
Course code:
TUD01
Minimum year of study:
3
Language of tutition:
English
 
Prerequisites:

Bachelor courses in calculus, linear algebra, differential equations and computer programming;

For this course a basic knowledge of English is indispensable.



Objectives:

The objective of this course is to introduce students to the mathematical modelling and numerical simulation of vibrations of structures. The finite element method will be introduced on a set of problems of increasing complexity.


Programme to be followed:

Day 1: mass-spring-damper system, sets of interconnected points masses

Day 2: rigid body mechanism;

Day 3: linear elastic medium;


Day 4: finite element modeling;


Day 5: company visit and student presentations;

Course exam:

Course assessment is based on active participation in the course
