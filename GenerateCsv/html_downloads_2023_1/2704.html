Objectives:

1.Within the frame of electrostatic to relate electromagnetic field to its sources.

2.Maxwell equations in the vacuum. 

3.Introduction to electromagnetic waves.

Programme to be followed:
There will be some technical courses focusing on the components of foods, and also a market survey will be done to let the students learn about the composition of different food materials. Students are expected to perform a project related with the evaluation of individual components of foods, such as water, carbohydrates, proteins, lipids, enzymes, and vitamins giving particular attention to their chemical structures and reactions and to the role of each component on food quality.
