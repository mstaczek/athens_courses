Objectives:

By introducing soft materials in the design of robotic systems, they become safe in interaction with humans and other delicate objects. However, their analysis doesn’t fit the traditional hard robotic framework. In this course you will get a broad introduction to the field for soft robotics, where you will learn how to design, fabricate and control these new types of robots, and learn how to apply them in various scenarios. By the end of this project you will have acquired the necessary skills and knowledge to create your own inflatable soft robots and be familiar with some of the state-of-the-art research topics in the soft robotics domain.

Programme to be followed:

Before the ATHENS week:

A small practical assignment will be given 2 weeks before. 

Based on their results, interactions will be stimulated between the students. Based on the lessens-learned from their own experiments and the experiments of others, students will be asked to repeat the first assignment and reflect their results a second time online.  

During the course week:

Monday-Thursday: 6 hours a day, a mixture between theory, practice and group discussion

Friday: 3 hours, meeting the experts seminar

After the ATHENS week:

Write a report (deadline 31/03/2023)

Course exam:
Continuous evaluation and a graded assignment after the course week
