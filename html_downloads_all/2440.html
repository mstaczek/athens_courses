Course title:
Introduction to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
Course code:
ITU INF21
Minimum year of study:
3
Language of tutition:
English
 
Prerequisites:
Calculus I and II, Introductory Course on Probability Theory
Objectives:

Data Analysis, Data Mining, Pattern Recognition, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence

The main goals of the course are:
1.To introduce artificial intelligence concepts.

2.To introduce fundamental machine learning methods.

3.To introduce weak and strong AI concepts along with intelligent agents.

4. To teach how to apply data analysis models to the data collected from different sources.

5.To teach basic probability theory concepts and their applications on machine learning approaches.

6. To introduce accountability of AI systems.
Programme to be followed:

1. Intelligent Agents

2. Weak and Strong AI

3. Notes on Probability Theory

4. Bayes Decision Rule

5. Linear Discriminant Analysis

6. Support Vector Machines

7. Neural Networks and Deep Learning Approaches

8. Accountable AI
