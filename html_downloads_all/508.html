Prerequisites:

Objectives:
Exploring future avenues of science and technology in a long range society and industry perspective. The course complements the innovation management courses delivered in management and engineering curricula. It provides students in management and engineering with an opportunity to explore together critical issues and areas of interactions of science, industry, and society.
