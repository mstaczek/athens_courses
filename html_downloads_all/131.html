Course title:
Introduction to Seismics
Course code:
TUD8
Minimum year of study:
3
Language of tutition:
English
 
Prerequisites:
All students who are in their third year of a BSc program can attend. Knowledge of the Matlab® environment is helpful but not necessary.
Objectives:
The most important aspects of reflection seismology are treated: sources and receivers of seismic energy and recording systems (data acquisition), and data sorting, seismic wave velocity analysis, data stacking and image forming through migration (data processing).
Programme to be followed:
Fifteen hours of lectures are scheduled in the mornings and twelve hours of practical sessions using the Matlab® environment are scheduled in the afternoons. A half day field exercise is planned in the second day of the week. During the first practical the Matlab® environment is explored using simple seismic examples.
