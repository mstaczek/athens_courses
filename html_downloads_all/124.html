Course title:
Computer-Aided Electronic Design: Diseño Electrónico Asistido por Computador
Course code:
UPM 22
Minimum year of study:
3
Language of tutition:
English / Spanish
 
Prerequisites:
Bachelor level in Sciences, Electronic Engineering, Physics, Computer Engineering, Telecommunication Engineering.
Objectives:
  • To present the tools used in electronic design.
  • To give an overview of the VHDL language.
  • To present the architecture and functioning of FPGAs.
  • To practice the fundamentals of electronic design in a real platform with several examples
Programme to be followed:
  • General concepts of computer-aided electronic design.
  •  Introduction to VHDL.
  • FPGA-based design.
  • Laboratory.

Practical exercises and written exam on last course day
