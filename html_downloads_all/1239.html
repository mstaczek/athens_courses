Course title:
[CANCELLED] Quantum entanglement for communications: from theory to experiments
Course code:
TPT18
Minimum year of study:
4
Language of tutition:
English
 
Prerequisites:
Maxwell's equations
Objectives:

Quantum entanglement is the basic ressource for the future quantum relays or

repeaters. The objective of this course is to acquire a thorough understanding of this concept from the theoretical definition to the practical implementation of entangled photons states, using non linear optics and to see how it can be used in various quantum communications devices.

Programme to be followed:

Basic quantum physics

Entanglement, EPR paradox, Field quantization, beamsplitters

Introduction to nonlinear optics (second order nonlinear phenomena)

Entangled photons: polarization, time-energy, time-bin

Physical implementation of entangled photon pairs sources

Quantum teleportation, entanglement swapping

Quantum cryptography protocols using entangled states

Labwork on entangled photon pair sources and nonlinear optics (3 hours)

Quantum memories
