Course title:
Introduction to the Structural Vibration Control Systems
Course code:
TUM 1121-02
Minimum year of study:
5
Language of tutition:
English
 
Prerequisites:

Structural Dynamics

and/or

Fundamentals of Earthquake Engineering
Objectives:

Learning the basics of structural vibration control systems and their application to mitigate the risk of earthquakes.

The main objective of this course is to make the students familiar by the fundamental concepts of earthquake engineering, vibration control systems, and their application to the building for response mitigation under earthquake excitation.

 
Programme to be followed:
Virtual lectures of approximately 3 - 4 hours per day will be divided in the mornings and afternoons.

Submitting a defined project latest 2 weeks after the end of the course.
