Course title:
Musique, Science et Histoire
Course code:
MP12
Minimum year of study:
2
Language of tutition:
Français - French
 
Prerequisites:
Know how to read a score. It is essential to review an elementary music theory textbook before the beginning of the course.
Objectives:
Through the example of music, without forgetting its historical dimension, make students understand the interactions that artistic practices and the sciences and techniques associated with it can have.
